Technique Description Water Savings Cost

Low-Flow Fixtures Installing low-flow showerheads and faucets. 30-40% reduction in water usage. Moderate ($50-$200)
Greywater Recycling Reusing water from sinks and showers for irrigation. Up to 60% reduction in water usage. High ($1,000-$5,000)
