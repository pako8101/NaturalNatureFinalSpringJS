Types of Herbal Remedies: Teas, Tinctures, Oils, and More

Herbal remedies come in many forms, each with unique preparation methods and uses:

  • Teas: Steep herbs like chamomile or peppermint in hot water for a soothing drink.
  • Tinctures: Concentrated extracts like echinacea, taken in small doses.
  • Oils: Essential oils like lavender, used for aromatherapy or topical application.
  • Salves: Herbal balms for skin healing, such as calendula salve.
